<td><p>Newsletter2Go</p>
(release 06/19)<br />

<p><br />
</p></td>
<td><div class="content-wrapper">
<p>The plugin newsletter2go can be activated in the configuration settings.</p>
<p><img src="attachments/23561075/23571155.png" class="confluence-embedded-image" height="150" /></p>
<p>eZ Commerce and eZ Commerce Advanced support the standard attributes of Newsletter2Go as e-mail address, name, gender, telephone number, date of birth, etc.</p>
<p>Additional attributes for shopping behavior are also supported as date of last order, value of last order, total value of all orders of the user.</p>
<pre class="tw-data-text tw-ta tw-text-small"><code>The following Newsletter2Go functions are supported: </code></pre>
<ul>
<li>Subscribe to newsletter via a form box</li>
<li>Subscribe to newsletter while registering in the shop</li>
<li>Subscribe to and unsubscribe from the user profile</li>
<li>Change newsletter details in the user profile of the shop</li>
<li>User data is updated during the checkout process (total order amount, date of order)</li>
<li>After the login in the shop, the newsletter information is obtained in real time from Newsletter2Go and updated in the shop profile</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Advanced version only</p>
<p>Customer Center</p></td>
<td><div class="content-wrapper">
<p>The customer-center offers B2B advantages especially for large customers.</p>
<p>Via the user management feature, customers are able to grant their own employees access to the shop. It improves the purchasing process since it offers features such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>Budget management</li>
<li>Approval functions for orders</li>
<li>Different roles and rights</li>
</ul>
<p><img src="attachments/23561075/23568301.png" class="confluence-embedded-image" height="400" /></p>
<p><img src="attachments/23561075/23568313.png" class="confluence-embedded-image" height="400" /></p>
<p>The customer center can be connected to the ERP system and synchronizes customer data and contact data when required. In that case the ERP also controls which customer can use the customer center. </p>
<p>If a customer has a customer center all employees will be stored beneath his company folder. The shop administrator will have a good overview of the company and its related employees with a shop account. </p>
<p>The customer center will enhance the eZ Commerce processes such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>Registration</li>
<li>Login</li>
<li>Checkout</li>
<li>Customer center management</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>

<td>Olark Chat Tool</td>
<td><div class="content-wrapper">
<p>Olark is a powerful chat tool which offers more than a normal chat. The shop is able to forward the name of a user (if logged in) to the service employee. Besides a chat functionality the service employee can assist the customer e.g. in pointing him to the correct place in the shop ("!push /Produkt-Katalog/Mikrofone/URM-Serie"). If no support employee is available the conversion will be send by email. </p>
<img src="http://confluence.extranet.silversolutions.de:8090/download/attachments/29305015/image2015-12-17%2021%3A26%3A40.png?version=1&amp;modificationDate=1450385321000&amp;api=v2" class="confluence-embedded-image confluence-thumbnail confluence-external-resource" height="150" /><img src="http://confluence.extranet.silversolutions.de:8090/download/attachments/29305015/image2015-12-17%2021%3A40%3A1.png?version=1&amp;modificationDate=1450385321000&amp;api=v2" class="confluence-embedded-image confluence-thumbnail confluence-external-resource" height="150" /><img src="http://confluence.extranet.silversolutions.de:8090/download/attachments/29305015/image2015-12-17%2021%3A50%3A11.png?version=1&amp;modificationDate=1507876013000&amp;api=v2" class="confluence-embedded-image confluence-thumbnail confluence-external-resource" height="150" />
<p>The chat tool can be enabled in the configuration settings of eZCommerce.</p>
<p><img src="attachments/23561075/23571142.png" class="confluence-embedded-image" height="250" /></p>
<p>Please not that you need a valid license from Olark which might cause extra costs.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Advanced version only</p>
<p>Delegate function</p>
<p><br />
</p></td>
<td><div class="content-wrapper">
<p>The goal of the delegate function is that a special user can delegate to another user, means that he can move in the shop with a different customer number.</p>
<p>This feature is an important function for sales persons. They can use the delegate feature to support a customer remotly or to prepare a meeting with the customer. </p>
<p><img src="attachments/23561075/23568163.png" class="confluence-embedded-image" height="400" /></p>
</td>

<tr>
<td><p>Rating &amp; Review</p>
<p>(release 06/19)<br />
</p></td>
<td><div class="content-wrapper">
<p>Customers can submit ratings and product reviews. Shop operators can build up trust As customers tend to trust the judgment of other customers.  Online shops with product reviews can rank higher in search engines which leads to more traffic.</p>
<ul>
<li>Storage of ratings and reviews in the shop</li>
<li>Simple administration and export of reviews in the back end</li>
<li>Filter and report options for reviews</li>
</ul>
<p><img src="attachments/23561075/23567526.png" class="confluence-embedded-image" height="250" /></p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>Voucher</p>
<p>(release 06/19)<br />
</p></td>
<td><p>Discount or value vouchers can easily be created in the shop back end. A vouchers contains discount conditions like minimum basket value or discountable products. Create individual vouchers that are valid a given number of times for a certain time period or personalised vouchers that are only valid once.</p>
<ul>
<li>Easy creation of vouchers in the back end</li>
<li>Different types of vouchers available</li>
<li>Settlement with the ERP system</li>
</ul></td>
